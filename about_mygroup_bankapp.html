About MyGroup BankApp

This is a SmartTech++ record-keeping and financial accounting tool for village bank and similar systems.

It supports the analysis and organisation of:

  1. Contributions data.
  2. Membership data.
  3. Interest calculations.
  4. Group account balance calculations.
  5. Group debtor's statistics.
and is able to export systematic PDF documents.

The app is powered by the secure and reliable Google Firebase Database Management System for its authentication, data storage and organisation.

GUIDE

After installation and account opening, the Treasurer creates a group by signing in as Treasurer. Then the other members join the group by signing in as Other and selecting their Treasurer's email. And the Treasurer, the group's Admin, add or approves the group members' registration.

Changing a Group's Treasurer

The current Treasurer should go to Members, then edit the New Treasurer's position from Other to Treasurer.
Finally, he/she should edit their own position from Treasurer to Other.

Starting a New Banking Phase

After dividing shares, the Treasurer should go to Settings and Reset Accounts to delete all financial data. Then the group account is ready for a new start.
