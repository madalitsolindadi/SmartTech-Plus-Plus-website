About Mpemba SDASongs App

Mpemba SDASongs App was made in love for "Church Music" and the Mpemba SDA Church Singing Groups by Madalitso P. Lindadi. a solo software app developer.

The app is a smart song book system containing songs for Mpemba SDA Church singing groups.
It enables easy lyric access through simple text/number search and screen navigations.
Additionally you can edit and add new songs.
