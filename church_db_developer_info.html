About SDA Church Database App

This is a SmartTech++ database application system that supports some of the operations carried out by the Clerks, Treasurers and Jesus' Way leaders (Elders) of the Seventh-Day Adventist Church.

It supports the analysis and organisation of:

  1. Church membership data.
  2. Jesus' Way operations data.
  3. Tithe returners statistics.
and is able to export systematic PDF documents.

The app is powered by the secure and reliable Google Firebase Database Management System for its authentication, data storage and organisation.

GUIDE

After installation and account opening, the Church Clerk creates a church account by signing in as Clerk. Then the other church members register with the church by signing in as Other and selecting their Clerk's email. And the Clerk, the church's account Admin, add or approves the church members' registration.

Changing a Church Clerk

The current Clerk should go to Church Members, then edit the New Clerk's position from Other to Clerk, email to the Church email.
Finally, he/she should edit their own position from Clerk to Other, the Church email to any personal email. Then log out and create an account using that personal email.

Changing Other Church Positions

The current Clerk should go to Church Members, then edit the current positions to the new positions.
