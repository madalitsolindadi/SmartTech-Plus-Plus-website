About SDA Church Database App

This is a SmartTech++ database system based on some of the operations carried out by the Clerks, Treasurers and Jesus' Way leaders (Elders) of the Seventh-Day Adventist Church.

It is powered by the secure and reliable Google Firebase Database system for it's authentication, data storage and organization.
